Roadmap

At PrimeTek, we are passionate about improving PrimeNG and would like to share our ideas for 2023 with the community. These are planned to be implemented in parallel to the regular maintenance work of the library involving review of issue tickets, PRs and PrimeNG PRO support.

Finalize WCAG Level AA compliance for all components with screen reader and keyboard support based on community feedback and audit tools.

New Design Tokens

Introduce new design tokens for styled mode.

Implement design tokens as CSS variables instead of SCSS.

New Styled Mode
